About the Project

This project was done in a group of 6 and we focused on how gas and oil prices have changed since 1990. The part of the project that I was in charge of was how conflicts in the Middle East affected oil business stock and consumer gas prices.


The graph on the right depicts the major oil companies of Shell, BP, and Exxon mapped with the shaded areas showing the major Middle Eastern conflicts.
