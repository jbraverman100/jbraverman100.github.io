About the Project

This was a semester long group project to find a company and analyze one of their managerial challenges. The group I was a part of chose Blackberry to focus on. We did a survey and interviews with some of the employees to better understand the company. This project culminated with a 12 page paper and a presentation for the class at the end of the semester.


The graphs shown are how satisfied the employees are with the current leadership and performance (top) and satisfaction with goal communication (bottom)
